ABOUT SYDNEY OPEN RESEARCH

Open science and open research practices are growing in Australia, but discovering open research can feel like an isolating experience. This is a mechanism to promote open science and open practices in New South Wales, to build a sense of community amongst people who might otherwise feel like the only open researchers they know.
We aim to help you find like-minded folks, support efforts to reform institutional policies and practices, and educate others about open research. Follow us on twitter for updates. Sydney Open Research is an inclusive and welcoming community by design. To preserve this, our activities are subject to our Code of Conduct. Please familiarise yourself with it.
